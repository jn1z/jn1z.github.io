Permuse
What is Permuse?
🛈

Reach the target word in the fewest number of moves.

Every day new words will be posted (along with commentary on the word choice).

Each move must be a valid dictionary word of the same length, and is either a one-letter edit or a permutation of the previous move.

Move colors indicate progress towards the target, in terms of the minimum distance remaining. Blue is positive, gray is no progress, and red is negative.

You can delete previous move(s), but they count against your total guesses.

Example

Source is "cat", and target is "dog".

cot

would be a good start. In fact, "dot" or "cog" would then solve the puzzle.

con

would be a neutral (unnecessary) guess after "cot". Playing "dot" or "cog" would still solve the puzzle.

can

after "cot" would be negative progress. It would take an extra step to get back. Better to delete it!
